How it works

Three seconds from thought to text.

1

Hold Ctrl+Space

A floating pill appears. Your mic is live.

2

Speak naturally

Talk like you're texting a friend. ChatFlow handles punctuation, formatting, and cleanup.

3

Release → text appears

Your words land right where your cursor is. In any app. No switching windows.

Pro tip

Hold Shift too → Enhanced mode

ChatFlow reads your screen to get technical terms, project names, and jargon right. Perfect for code reviews, bug reports, and technical writing.

Features

Built different. Built to disappear.

Smart formatting

Adapts to the app you're in.

Discord? Casual, light punctuation. Email? Proper sentences. Code editor? Preserves technical terms. Terminal? Raw output. ChatFlow detects the active app and adjusts.

Vocabulary

Learns your words.

Edit a transcription and ChatFlow remembers. Next time you say "ChatFlow" instead of "chat flow" — it just knows.

Zero setup

Sign in with ChatGPT.

No API keys. No billing. Sign in with your ChatGPT account (free plan works) and start talking.

Privacy

Audio streams directly to OpenAI. Never stored.

Your voice goes to OpenAI's Realtime API and back as text. ChatFlow never records, stores, or logs your audio. Enhanced mode screenshots are processed in memory, never saved to disk.
