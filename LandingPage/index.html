flow mode

Feels like a polished dictation tool. Because that is the bar.

The front half of ChatFlow is intentionally boring in the best way: hotkey, waveform, transcript, done. The back half is where the side quest starts.

the receipts

Your ChatGPT plan is the payment rail. Codex OAuth is the side entrance.

We keep the hack visible on purpose, because that is the interesting bit for builders. The page is allowed to look professional. The proof still needs to be legible.

01 sign in

Open the ChatGPT auth flow.

ChatFlow mirrors the Codex CLI PKCE dance, including the local callback server and verifier exchange.

/oauth/authorize -> localhost callback
02 keep the token legit

Exchange code, store refresh token.

The callback code becomes a usable access token. Refresh material lives in Keychain, not in some cursed plaintext file.

grant_type=authorization_code
03 stream audio

Ship PCM16 to Realtime and wait for text.

Mic audio streams over WebSocket, transcription deltas arrive, and the final text gets injected into the app you started in.

input_audio_buffer.append
works today, not promised forever

This is still a third-party path. Auth details can move. That is part of the charm and part of the risk, so the site should feel honest about both.
